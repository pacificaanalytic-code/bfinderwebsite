Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 1, 2026  ·  BFinder LLC

This policy describes how BFinder LLC collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the BFinder app.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BFinder. BFinder LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BFinder mobile application (the "App"), a marketplace connecting mobile beauty professionals ("Experts") with clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By creating an account or using any feature of the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the App, and information from third-party services. Account Information • Full name, email address, phone number, and profile photo. • Role selection (Client or Expert) and, for Experts, business name, bio, and portfolio images. Location Data • Approximate or precise device location, collected with your permission, to surface nearby Experts and to enable service-area matching. • For Experts, the service radius you set in your profile. Booking & Transaction Data • Services selected, appointment dates and times, and in-app messages. The platform does not process payments; we do not store raw card numbers. Usage & Device Data • Device identifiers, operating system, app version, crash logs, and feature-interaction events. Camera • The App requests camera permission solely to scan QR codes for invitation redemption. We do not capture, store, or transmit any photos or video from your camera. Push Notification Tokens • If you grant permission, we store a push notification token to deliver appointment reminders and in-app alerts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: • Create and maintain your account. • Match Clients with Experts based on location, services, and availability. • Facilitate connections and munications between Clients and Experts. • Send transactional notifications (booking confirmations, reminders) and, with your consent, marketing messages. • Respond to support requests and resolve disputes. • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents. • Analyse aggregate usage patterns to improve the App. • ply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances: Between Users • When a Client books an Expert, limited profile information (name, photo, and rating) is shared to facilitate the appointment. Service Providers • We engage trusted third-party vendors (cloud hosting, push-notification services, analytics providers) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations. Legal Requirements • We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority. Business Transfers • If BFinder LLC is involved in a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with prior notice.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, your profile is hidden immediately and permanently deleted after a 14-day grace period, except where retention is required by law.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for data at rest, and role-based access controls. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, but we are mitted to protecting your information.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to: • Access – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. • Correction – Update inaccurate information via your profile settings. • Deletion – Delete your account in Settings → Danger Zone. • Portability – Request a machine-readable export of your data. • Opt-out of marketing – Unsubscribe via the link in any email. • Location / Camera – Withdraw permissions at any time in your device settings. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@bfinder..

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@bfinder.com and we will delete that information.
