Why BFinder

Everything you need,
all in one app.

From discovering new talent to reconnecting with your go-to stylist — BFinder makes it effortless.

Find Nearby Experts

Discover beauty professionals in your neighborhood using real-time location. See exactly how far away they are on an interactive map.

Search by Specialty

Filter by expertise — hair styling, barber, makeup, nails, extensions, and more. Find exactly the skill you need, fast.

Follow Your Favorites

Follow the experts you love and receive push notifications when they share announcements, discounts, or new services. Never miss an update.

Portfolio & Reference Photos

Browse expert work galleries and upload reference photos for inspiration. Experts showcase their best work with a full visual portfolio.

Map View

See all available experts plotted on a live map. Zoom in, tap a pin, and see their full profile — all without leaving the map.

Ratings & Reviews

Clients can leave star ratings and written reviews on expert profiles. Transparent, verified feedback so you always know who you're working with.
